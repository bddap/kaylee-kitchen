Andrea's Enchiladas

From the kitchen of

Andrea Heikkila

serves 8
ready in 35
2 cups chicken breast
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 can of green chilies
1 Jalapeno
1 onion
1 small container of sour cream
1 cup monterey jack cheese
Corn tortillas
Steps

Combine chicken, 1 can cream of chicken soup, green chilies, jalapeno and onion into bowl and mix

Optional fry the corn tortillas

Roll the mix into the tortillas

Combine sour cream and last can cream of chicken into another bowl and place on top of rolled tortillas

Bake in the oven at 350 for 30 minutes

Grate cheese onto dish and bake for 5 more minutes.

Garnish with cilantro
