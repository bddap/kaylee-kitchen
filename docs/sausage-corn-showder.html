Sausage Corn Showder

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
1 pound bulk pork sausage
1 cup coarsely chopped onion
4 cups peeled potatoes cut in 1/2-inch cubes 2 cups water
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1 17 ounce can cream-style corn
1 17 ounce can whole-kernel corn, drained
1 12 ounce can evaporated milk
Steps

1. In a Dutch oven or kettle, cook the pork sausage and onion until the sausage is brown and the onion is tender. Drain mixture on paper towels.

2. Return sausage mixture to Dutch oven. Stir in potatoes, water, salt, and pepper. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, about 15 minutes or until potato is just tender.

3. Add the cream-style corn, whole-kernel corn, and the evaporated milk. Heat through. Makes 6 serv- ings.
