Rum Cake

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
Cake:
1 package yellow cake mix
1 small instant vanilla pudding mix 4 eggs
1/2 cup cold water
1/2 cup rum
1/2 cup vevetable oil
Rum Glaze:
1/4 lb (1 stick) butter 1/4 lb water
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup rum
Steps

Preheat oven 325 degrees F.

Grease a bundt pan liberally with cooking spray. Then add a few tablespoons of granulated sugar to the inside of the pan, and gently tap and turn it around until every square inch of the inside of the pan is coated in sugar. Set aside.

Mix pudding, cake mix, eggs water, rum and oil until smooth.

Pour into pan and Bake 1 hour

For Glaze:

Melt butter in sauce pan and stir in the water and sugar. Boil for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the rum.

Cool in pan for 25 minutes.

Invert cake onto serving plate.

Prick top and spoon and brush the rum glaze evenly over cake.
