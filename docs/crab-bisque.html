Crab Bisque

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves 4
ready in 20
Half a cooked crab
⅓ Celery stalk & celery leaves
2 Carrots
1 Onion
Spices: Paprika, salt, pepper, garlic salt, old bay seasoning, 1 bay leaf, cloves all to taste
⅓ cup Heavy Cream
¾ cup Flour
1 cup Milk
Half a stick of Butter
Pinch of fresh Parsley
Steps

Remove crab meat and set aside. Boil the crab shells, half the celery, 1 carrot and half the onion, minced in 2 ⅕ cups of water and 1 can chicken broth for 30 minutes.

Strain and squeeze the shells and vegetables into the pot. Remove and set aside.

Add the crab meat,remaining veggies, spices, parsley, milk and butter, stirring often.

Once boiling, add flour with a bit of milk.

Serve hot, with crusty bread, top with dried parsley flakes.
