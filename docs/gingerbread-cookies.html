Gingerbread cookies

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
Dry ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp ground allspice
1 egg
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar 2/3 cup dark molasses
6 Tbsp softened butter
Steps

-Sift together dry ingredients

-mix with other ingredients

-cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.

-Preheat oven to 350 degrees F

-Roll out onto floured board to 1/4 inch thick -cut into cookies.

-Bake for 8 min.

Makes about 50 cookies.
