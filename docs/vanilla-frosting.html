Vanilla Frosting

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves 0
ready in 0
3 Cups powdered sugar
1 1/2 Tsp Vanilla
2 TBS Milk
1/3 cup softened butter
In a mixing bowl, combine the softened butter with the powdered sugar.
Mix them together using a mixing spoon or electric mixer set on low speed until they are well blended.2
Add the vanilla extract and one teaspoon of the milk and mix well.
Scrape the sides of the bowl often while mixing.3
Continue to add milk one teaspoon at a time until frosting is the desired consistency.4
Blend until the icing is smooth and creamy with no lumps.5
Frost your cake, cupcakes or desert, or refrigerate to use later.
