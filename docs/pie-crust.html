Pie Crust

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter, chilled and diced 1/4 cup ice water
Steps

In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Cut in but- ter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in water, a tablespoon at a time, until mixture forms a ball. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.

Roll dough out to fit a 9 inch pie plate. Place crust in pie plate. Press the dough evenly into the bottom and sides of the pie plate.
