Raspberry Filled Cookies

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
Cookie base:
1 1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking powder 1/4 tsp salt
1/4 lb butter
1/4 cup powdered sugar 1 tsp vanilla
1 Tbsp whipped cream
Top ring of cookie:
2 1/4 cup almond flour 1 cup sugar
1 Tbsp corn starch
1 large egg
1/2 tsp almond extract
Raspberry jam
1 egg white to brush on top of cookie
Steps

-Combine and mix ingredients for cookie base and the top ring of the cooking. Let both sit for 1 hour.

-roll out base and cut out circles

-push the almond top around the top of the edge of the base.

-fill the center with Raspberry jam

-brush top of cookie with egg white.

-Bake in 350 degree oven for 8 - 10 min.
