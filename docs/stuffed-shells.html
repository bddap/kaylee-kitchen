Stuffed Shells

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
Shell Filling:
30 oz Ricatta cheese
12 oz Mozzarella cheese 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese 2 eggs
1 Tbsp Chopped parsley 1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Cheese Sprinkle:
3 Tbsp Parmesan cheese 4 oz Mozzarella cheese
Sauce:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup onion chopped 1 clove garlic chopped
2 lb can whole tomatoes 6 oz can tomatoe paste 8 oz can tomatoe sauce 2 Tbsp Parsley
1 - 2 Tbsp sugar 1 tsp Oragano 1/2 tsp basil
1/4 tsp pepper
Steps

For Sauce:

-Saute onion and garlic in olive oil until tender. -add other sauce ingredients and cook for 1 hour.

For Filling:

-Mix ricatta, Mozzarella and parmesan cheese, eggs, parsley, salt and pepper.

For Shells:

-Boil according to pachage directions. -Fill with cheese filling

-sprinkle with parmesan mozzarella mix

-cover with remaining sauce
