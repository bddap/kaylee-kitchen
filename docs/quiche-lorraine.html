Quiche Lorraine

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
1 pie crust in a 9 inch pie pan
3 eggs
1 1/3 cups half-and-half or whole milk
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
4 strips cooked bacon
2 cups grated cheese (your favorite kind, Lorraine, Swiss cheese)
Steps

-Preheat oven to 375 degrees F

-Crumble the bacon and sprinkle it in the pie shell. Sprinkle on about half of the grated cheese.

-Ladle the egg mixture over the filling, then sprinkle on the remaining cheese.

-Bake at 375 on the center oven rack until slightly puffy and golden brown, about 40 to 45 minutes.

Serve warm, at room temperature, or cold. Makes 8 servings.
