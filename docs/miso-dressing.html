Miso Dressing

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
3 tablespoons neutral oil (such as peanut, canola, vegetable, etc).
1 1/2 tablespoons rice vinegar
2 teaspoons white miso
1/4 teaspoon (a drop) sesame oil 1/2 teaspoon honey or maple syrup 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
Steps

Combine the ingredients in a bowl and whisk until smooth, adding a dash more vinegar or honey to taste. Feel free to spice it up with some Sriracha, a hit of hot pepper flakes, some grated fresh ginger or even a teaspoon of peanut butter.
