Beef and Pumpkin Curry

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves 4
ready in
1 cup chuck roast beef, chopped into 1 inch strips 1Tbsp oil
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
1 cup fresh pumpkin (or potatoes) peeled and cubed 2 cups canned coconut milk
1 medium Aneheim chile, chopped
3 Tbsp red curry paste (or to taste)
1/2 cup basil or cilantro, chopped
Steps

Brown meat on high heat and soften onions in the oil.

Turn heat to low and mix in curry paste.

Stir in coconut milk, cover and simmer for 15 - 20 min.

-Add pumpkin (or potatoes )and cook over medium heat until pumkin is tender.

Serve by ladling mixture over bowls of rice. Garnish with basil or cilantro and add chiles to taste.

(If using powdered curry, start by warming up curry powder in a dry pan for a minute or two, then con- tinue with teh meat and onions as above.)
