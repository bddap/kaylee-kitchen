Filet of Sole Meunière

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
4 Sole, cod or hallibut filet 3 Tbsp four
7 oz clarified butter
2 Tbsp lemon juice
4 Tbsp chopped parsley lemon wedges
Steps

Pat the fish dry with paper towels

Dust lightly with flour and season with salt and pepper

Heat 5 oz of butter in a frying pan (if large enough to fit fish, if not use half the butter and cook in 2 batches)

Put the fish in the pan and cook for 4 minutes on each side or until golden.

Lift the fish out onto warm plates and drizzle with lemon juice and parsley.

Add the remaining butter to the pan and heat until it browns to make a beurre noisett. Pour over the fish (it will foam as it mixes with the lemon juice) and serve with lemon wedges.
