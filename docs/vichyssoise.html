Vichyssoise

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
5 cups peeled, chopped all-pupose potatoes
3 cups thinly sliced leeks (use the white and 2 inches of the green)
1 2/3 cup chicken broth
5 cups water
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 tsp pepper (white if you want to stay white)
1/2 cup heavy cream
chopped chives (optional)
Steps

1. In soup pot, add the potatoes, leeks, broth, water, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 1 hour.

2. Puree the soup and pour it into a large bowl. Stir in the cream. Serve warm or cold.

Makes 6 - 8 servings
