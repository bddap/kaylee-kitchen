Souper Yammy Soup

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
• 2 cooked, skinless chicken breasts sliced
• 1 med. yellow onion
• 1 serrano chille sliced
• 2 garlic cloves chopped
• 1⁄2 red pepper sliced in strips • 2 tbs olive oil
• 4 cups chicken broth
• 1 med. yam (sliced in strips)
• 15 oz. canned hominy drained • sliced cooked chicken breast
• 14 oz unsweetened canned coconut milk/cream • 2 plum tomatoes sliced and seeded
Toppings
• 1/3 cup avocado sliced
• Fresh squeezed lime juice • cilantro
Steps

Sauté in a soup pot for 5-10 min:

onion, serrano chille sliced, garlic, red pepper in the olive oil

Add to soup pot and cook on med just till boiling:

chickens broth, yam, hominy, chicken breast

Add to soup pot and serve:

coconut milk and plum tomatoes salt and pepper to taste

Don’t forget the yummy toppings on the side:

avacado, lime and cilantro
