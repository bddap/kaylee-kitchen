Pork Tenderloin with Honey Mustard BBQ Sauce

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
Pork Tenderloin:
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 pork tenderloins (3/4 - 1 pound each), trimmed Coarse salt and ground pepper
Honey Mustard Sauce: 1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup dijon mustard 1/4 cup honey
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar 1/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
Steps

Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Season pork with salt and pepper; transfer to skillet and cook, turning, until browned all over, 5 to 10 minutes.

Reduce heat to medium-low and cover skillet; cook pork, turning occasionally, until an instant-read thermometer registers 150 degrees. 20 to 25 min- utes.

Remove pork from skillet, wrap in aluminum foil, and let rest 10 minutes (reserve skillet with juices).

Slice pork thinly and serve with Honey Mustard sauce.

For Sauce:

1. Melt butter in a pan

2. Add other ingredients

3. Boil on med heat for 10 minutes.
