Banana Bread

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
3 medium bananas mashed 1/2 cup oil
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup chopped pecans
Steps

Preheat oven to 350

In a bowl, mix all ingredients by hand until well incorporated

Pour into a greased (and sprinkled with lots of sug- ar) bread pan (approx 9x4x4)

Bake 50 minutes to 1 hour or until browned and toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean (check at about 50 minutes)

Cool on rack
