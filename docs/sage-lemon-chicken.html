Sage Lemon Chicken

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
4 chicken breasts
Marinade:
3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice 5 Tbsp olive oil
28 sage leaves
3 Tbsp butter
salt and pepper
Steps

-Combine marinade and let chicken marinate for 1 hour.

-Heat 2 Tbsp oil and 3 Tbsp butter in a skillet. -add chicken and cook for 5 - 7min. on each side. -Remove chicken.

-add the fresh sage leaves to skillet and toast till crispy. Pour sage leaves and juices over the chicken.
