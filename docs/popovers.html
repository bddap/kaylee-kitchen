Popovers

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
2 Tbsp butter 2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup flour 1/4 tsp salt
extra butter, jam or maple syrup for the popovers
Steps

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

2. Brush the insides of muffin cups with melted butter.

3. mix eggs, milk, flour and salt.

4. pour batter into muffin cups (one-half to two- thirds full.

5. Bake for 30 minutes

6. Remove muffins from the pan and prick with a fork to let the steam escape. Spread with butter and/ or jam, and eat! (They also taste great dipped in maple syrup.)

Makes 1 dozen.
