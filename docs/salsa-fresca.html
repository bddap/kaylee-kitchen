Salsa Fresca

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves
ready in
6-8 plum tomatoes, cored and diced
1 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped
2-3 small cloves of garlic, minced (optional) 1 small white onion, skin removed and diced 1/2 bunch of cilantro, roughly chopped
1 lime, juiced
1/2 tsp salt
Steps

Combine diced tomatoes, jalapeño, garlic and onion in a medium bowl.

Add lime juice and salt and stir coat vegetables

Add in roughly chopped cilantro

Taste to see if extra salt or lime juice is needed

Set aside for at least 1/2 hour at room temperature to let ingredients meld together
