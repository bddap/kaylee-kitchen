Chinese Spicy Chicken

From the kitchen of

Dana Dirksen

serves 0
ready in 0
1 chicken breast
3 tbs flour
1 egg yolk
1 tbs of water
1 tbs soy sauce
1 teaspoon of sugar
2 cloves of garlic
2 tbs soy sauce
1 tbs of rice cooking wine
2 hot chili peppers
scallion
pinch of salt and pepper
Steps

Cut chicken breast into bite size chunk

marinade with egg yolk, flour , water, soy sauce for 15 minutes

deep fried until firm, and golden brown (but don't overcook)

saut√© garlic and chili pepper with low heat until fragrant add sugar (to caramelize)

add soy sauce, the wok should be smoky at this moment,

when it's fragrant, salt & pepper, scallions, wine, add chicken, cook until the chicken absorbs all the juice, serve
